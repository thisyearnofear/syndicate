# Product Ladder Brief

## Purpose
Define the clearest way to explain Syndicate without flattening distinct parts of the offering into one vague story.

## Recommended Ladder

### 1. Private Vaults
The hero product.

- privacy-native vault coordination
- selective disclosure
- best wedge for serious users, judges, and partners

### 2. Yield That Plays For You
The signature mechanic.

- deposit into Base-native vaults
- auto-convert earnings into tickets or causes
- strongest bridge between playful participation and capital efficiency

### 3. Public Play
The fast-entry mode.

- direct Megapot access through Syndicate
- easiest onboarding path
- useful top-of-funnel, but not the main identity

## Strategic Interpretation
Direct Megapot access is useful and should remain in the product.

However:
- it should support the overall system
- it should not dominate the product identity

The differentiator is not “we also have lottery access.”
The differentiator is:
- smarter participation through yield
- private coordination through Fhenix

## Product Rule
When in doubt:
- lead with Private Vaults
- support with Yield That Plays For You
- keep Public Play as optional fast entry
