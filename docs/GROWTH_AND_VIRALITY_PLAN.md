# Growth And Virality Plan

## Objective
Design growth loops that fit the product you actually have: a trust-based, privacy-native coordination product.

This is not a memecoin-style viral product. The right engine is:
- group formation
- invitation
- trust signaling
- repeated coordinated usage

## Core Growth Thesis
People will not share this because it is loud.
They will share it because it solves a coordination problem without overexposure.

That means Syndicate should optimize for:
- invite loops
- team or group adoption
- shareable proof of value
- strong demo moments

## Primary Growth Loop

### Loop 1. Create -> Invite -> Deposit -> Coordinate
1. one user creates a private vault / syndicate
2. they invite collaborators
3. collaborators deposit privately
4. the group experiences private coordination value
5. more groups form

This is the best near-term loop because it is already aligned with the product structure.

## Secondary Growth Loops

### Loop 2. Reveal -> Explain -> Refer
1. a user reveals their private balance in a demo or working session
2. that creates a memorable “how is that private?” moment
3. they share the product with another group or operator

### Loop 3. Coordinator adoption
1. a leader or treasury operator uses Syndicate for one group
2. they replicate the same setup for another working group, pool, or campaign
3. the product spreads through role-based reuse

## Virality Assets

### Demo asset
The strongest shareable artifact is not a dashboard screenshot. It is the before/after privacy moment:
- balance hidden
- authorized reveal
- exact value shown only to the user

### Marketing clips
Create very short clips for:
- encrypted deposit
- private balance reveal
- “public activity, private amount” explanation

### Shareable statements
- Your wallet should not reveal your conviction.
- Coordinate capital without exposing every position.
- Private by default. Verifiable when needed.

## Product Features That Strengthen Growth

### Highest value
- invite members into a private vault
- role-aware coordinator dashboard
- public vault / private vault comparison
- private participation explanation at onboarding

### Next wave
- controlled proof sharing
- reveal history
- contributor permission settings
- coordinator export or audit views

## Referral Model

### Recommended model
Do not force consumer-style referral gimmicks.

Instead, reward:
- group creators
- coordinators
- repeated vault launches
- successful team onboarding

### Suggested incentives
- featured private vault templates
- early access to advanced privacy modes
- ecosystem recognition
- curated showcase slots

## Go-To-Market Angles

### 1. Builders and judges
Angle:
`Privacy-native coordination built into a real product stack`

### 2. Users
Angle:
`Coordinate capital without exposing your balance`

### 3. Partners
Angle:
`Selective disclosure infrastructure for group finance`

## On-Product Growth Opportunities

### In create flow
After private vault creation:
- prompt: `Invite your first 3 members`

### In dashboard
After private reveal:
- lightweight callout:
  `Want your group to coordinate privately too? Create a private syndicate.`

### In syndicate pages
Add trust-building copy:
- `Amounts stay private`
- `Members reveal only what they choose`

## What To Avoid
- generic growth hacks
- consumer referral spam
- over-indexing on social virality instead of trust loops
- building “share” features that conflict with the privacy premise

## Metrics To Track

### Product metrics
- private vaults created
- private syndicates created
- invite acceptance rate
- deposits per vault
- repeat vault creators
- private balance reveal usage

### Narrative metrics
- demo completion rate
- homepage CTA conversion to private vault flow
- percentage of users choosing private vs public mode
